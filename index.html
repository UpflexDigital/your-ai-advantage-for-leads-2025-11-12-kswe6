Our founding team's combined 75+ years of sales and technology experience drives every feature we build and every decision we make.

We're committed to continuous innovation, customer success, and building a platform that doesn't just meet today's challenges but anticipates tomorrow's opportunities. Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI-powered lead generation platform.

How long does it take to see results with your platform?

Most customers see measurable results within the first 30 days of implementation. Our AI platform is designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing CRM and sales processes, allowing you to start identifying and engaging qualified leads immediately.

Within 90 days, our customers typically report a 340% increase in qualified leads, 60% reduction in sales cycle, and 45% lower customer acquisition costs. Of course, results vary based on your industry, sales process, and how quickly your team adopts the platform. We provide dedicated onboarding support to ensure you're maximizing value from day one.

Is the platform compatible with our existing CRM?

Yes, absolutely! Our platform integrates seamlessly with all major CRM systems including Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Microsoft Dynamics, and many others. We've built native integrations with the platforms used by 95% of B2B sales teams.

The integration process typically takes less than 30 minutes, and our technical team will guide you through every step. We also support custom API integrations if you use a specialized or legacy system. Your data remains secure and synchronized in real-time across all platforms.

How does your AI learn about our ideal customer profile?

Our machine learning algorithms analyze your historical data including closed deals, customer profiles, engagement patterns, and sales outcomes. Within the first week, the AI builds a comprehensive understanding of what constitutes your ideal customer profile.

The system continuously learns and improves its accuracy as it processes more data. You can also manually configure specific criteria like company size, industry, revenue, technology stack, and more. The AI then combines this manual input with behavioral data to identify prospects with 99.2% accuracy.

As your business evolves and your ICP changes, the platform automatically adapts. You'll receive regular insights about what's working and recommendations for refining your targeting strategy.

What security measures do you have in place?

Security is our top priority. We maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance, GDPR certification, and CCPA compliance. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. Our infrastructure is hosted on AWS with redundant backups and 99.99% uptime SLA.

We conduct regular third-party security audits and penetration testing. Your data is never shared with third parties without explicit consent, and you maintain complete control over who can access your information within your organization.

We also provide detailed audit logs, IP whitelisting, single sign-on (SSO) integration, and advanced user permission controls. If you have specific compliance requirements, our enterprise team can work with you to ensure full compliance.

What kind of support and training do you provide?

We provide comprehensive support including dedicated onboarding specialists, video training modules, live group training sessions, and detailed documentation. All customers receive 24/7 email support, and premium customers get priority phone support.

During your onboarding process, our team will work with you to configure your ideal customer profile, integrate your CRM, set up automation rules, and train your sales team on best practices. We also conduct quarterly business reviews to ensure you're achieving your goals and maximizing ROI.

Additionally, we have an active community forum, comprehensive knowledge base, and regular webinars featuring industry experts and top performers using our platform. Our success team is committed to your growth and will proactively identify opportunities for optimization.
